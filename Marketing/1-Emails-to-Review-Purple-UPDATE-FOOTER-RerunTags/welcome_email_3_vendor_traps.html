2
The "Demo Magic" Trap
Their demo works flawlessly with clean, structured data. Your real-world content? It's messy, complex, and doesn't fit their assumptions. But you won't discover this until after you've signed the contract.

Test with YOUR actual content before committing. Demand a proof-of-concept with your messiest, most complex data. If it can't handle your reality, it's not the right solution.

3
